The Architecture of Discovery

AURA/UPWARD is the Society for UAP Studies’ operational nervous system for research—a dual-track framework to guide rigorous inquiry, ensure long-term learning, and build a trusted institutional knowledge base on UAP.

What is AURA/UPWARD?

AURA/UPWARD is our integrated system guiding the full lifecycle of SUAPS-affiliated research. It ensures every project is rigorous, cumulative, and aligned with our mission to build trustworthy, interdisciplinary knowledge.

AURA

Accelerated UAP Research & Analysis

AURA is our governance engine. It evaluates and oversees structured research projects, focusing on design, execution, and accountability.

  • Evaluates proposals for alignment, feasibility, and rigor.
  • Assigns Research Readiness Levels (RRLs).
  • Oversees ethics, benchmarks, and project governance.
  • Manages logistics and project planning.

UPWARD

Unified Program for Weaving Anomaly Research & Discovery

UPWARD is our synthesis engine. It connects insights across all studies to ensure learning, coherence, and integration across the research ecosystem.

  • Synthesizes findings from diverse disciplines.
  • Produces thematic analyses and white papers.
  • Maintains the Institutional Knowledge Map (IKM).
  • Frames findings in broader epistemic contexts.

The SUAPS Research Lifecycle

Our framework follows a disciplined, 7-phase lifecycle adapted from the European Cooperation for Space Standardization (ECSS) to ensure clarity and accountability from concept to conclusion.

Phase 0: The Research On-Ramp

Curiosity-driven, exploratory ideas take root here. Once a concept matures, it may transition to AURA for formal review. This phase is generative, not operational.

Phase 1: Feasibility

AURA assesses the initial concept and defines its preliminary scope.

Phase 2: Definition

The project is defined in detail and aligned with funding opportunities.

Phase 3: Pre-Execution

Final logistics, team assembly, and resource allocation are locked in.

Phase 4: Execution

The core research is conducted according to the established plan.

Phase 5: Analysis & Publication

Data is analyzed, and findings are prepared for publication.

Phase 6: Integration & Closeout

UPWARD integrates findings into the IKM. The project is formally closed.

Key Distinction: Phase 0 is exploratory and donor-independent. AURA is selective and operational. UPWARD is integrative and institutional.

Why This Framework Matters

AURA/UPWARD is designed to build an enduring institution capable of making credible, cumulative progress on the UAP mystery.

Ensures Integrity

Prevents the fragmentation or loss of credible findings through a structured, transparent process.

Builds Trust

Strengthens our relationships with scholars, policymakers, and donors by demonstrating operational rigor.

Enables Sustainable Research

Creates a pipeline of well-defined, fundable research programs built for long-term success.

Creates Lasting Knowledge

Translates individual project findings into a coherent, institutional knowledge base that grows over time.

What AURA/UPWARD Is Not

  • It is not an intake for early-stage or unvetted ideas (that's Phase 0).
  • It is not a publication or a journal (though it supports both).
  • It is not a fundraising function (it informs development, but doesn't pitch).
  • It is not a repository for raw data or unvetted content.
  • It is not purely academic; it blends academic depth with nonprofit governance.

Who Makes It Work

This framework is managed by a dedicated team ensuring operational discipline and strategic alignment across the research ecosystem.

Director of Research Programs

Leads AURA, manages reviews, and tracks project milestones.

Director of Grants & Development

Oversees budgets and ensures alignment between projects and funding.

Director of Operations

Maintains tools, infrastructure, and phase discipline.

UPWARD Integration Lead

Maintains the Institutional Knowledge Map and ensures synthesis.

Editorial Liaison (Limina)

Connects UPWARD's synthesized insights to SUAPS's public outputs.

A System Built to Last

AURA/UPWARD is not a committee or a toolkit. It is the operational nervous system of SUAPS research—deciding what we fund, how we steward it, and what we learn. Like any trusted research body, we need both structure and synthesis. This is ours.
